About Me

Hi, I'm Gabe Maciejaszek! I'm a Year One student at RIT, and I'm majoring in Game Design and Development. Before RIT, though, I spent my entire life back home at Queens, New York. I know a lot of people are fascinated by the bright lights and such in the Big Apple, but personally, I grew tired of it as I got older. So, I began to search for a college far from home, but not too far. Obviously, the focus on game development this school offers sold me on it almost instantly, and I have to say I'm really happy I came here! Hopefully I feel the same way four years from today!

What Am I Trying to Make?

Growing up, I was absolutely in love with Playstation 2 games. In particular, I had a fondness for the platformer genre. Games like Ratchet and Clank, Jak and Daxter, and Sly Cooper and the Thievius Raccoonus made my imagination run wild. I became lost in these digital worlds every time I turned on the console, and in a way that's hard to explain, these games have shaped me into who I am. Because of this, I hold the people that made these games in high regard. I can only hope that one day I can inspire people the same way I was inspired as a kid.
