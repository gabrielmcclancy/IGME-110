I had a fairly limited selection of videos to choose from, seeing as stock video/image sites don't give away much in the way of quality content. So I decided to go for a more abstract kind of story, and narrate American life in around under a minute. I showed the perspective of various everyday citizens and then ended with a flag to paint the image of American freedom and opportunity. Cheesy, but I think it came out fairly decent.
