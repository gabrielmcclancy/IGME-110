Modern Warfare 2's Campaign: Good or Bad?

One of the most polarizing points of discussion between Call of Duty fans and Call of Duty naysayers is the campaign. Modern Warfare 2's campaign in particular seems to divide people the most. As mentioned in the essay, Call of Duty 4's campaign wanted to empower the player and allow them to live a power fantasy. This usually entailed big set pieces that took place throughout the many bits of gameplay. It was a logical progression for Infinity Ward to take this aspect of the campaign further, although those new to the series will likely see the moment-to-moment action and scoff.It looks like a Michael Bay movie and is void of any substance, say the haters.

I will allow you to be the judge by reading my essay as well as the blog post above and deciding which side seems more reasonable.
